Our Philosophy

This isn't a "business", it's a passion.

At Diamond Auto Spa Detailing, owner Andrew brings a completely different approach to auto detailing. Most detailing businesses rush through cars to maximize profits. But we see ourselves as artists, not just detailers. Here, your car will be treated like a mural: With precision, effort and genuine care.

Our Services

Every car tells a story and deserves individual attention.

Exterior Detailing

Complete exterior transformation including wash, clay bar treatment, polish, and premium protection. We restore your vehicle's exterior to showroom condition.

Interior Detailing

Deep cleaning of every interior surface, vacuum, steam cleaning, upholstery and carpet shampooing and leather conditioning. Special attention to pet hair removal.

Paint Correction

Professional multi-stage paint correction to eliminate swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation. We bring back your paint's original depth and brilliance.

Ceramic Coating

Long-lasting ceramic coating protection that provides superior shine and defense against environmental damage. A true investment in your vehicle.

Headlight Restoration

Transform cloudy, yellowed headlights back to crystal clear condition, improving both aesthetics and nighttime safety.

Engine Bay Detailing

Meticulous cleaning and dressing of your engine bay. We make the heart of your vehicle look as good as the exterior.
